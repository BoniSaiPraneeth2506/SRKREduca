Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our courses and services

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply navigate to our Courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click on the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the registration process where you can create an account (if you don't already have one) and complete the payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are secure and protected by industry-standard encryption.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. To apply for a scholarship, please visit the Scholarship section under About Us or contact our admissions team at scholarships@srkreduca.edu with your academic records and a statement of purpose.

Can I access course materials after completion?

Absolutely! Once you've enrolled in a course, you'll have lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates. This allows you to revisit the content whenever you need to refresh your knowledge.

How do I get technical support if I face issues?

For technical support, you can reach out to our dedicated support team via email at support@srkreduca.edu or call our technical helpline at +91 9876543210. Our team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM to assist you with any issues you might encounter.
